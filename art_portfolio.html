QIANWEI (JANE) ZHANG

EXPERIENCE PROJECTS ART PORTFOLIO ABOUT

As long as I could remember, I have always enjoyed expressing myself my making art. I've gone through phases of different mediums and art styles, such as color pencils, acrylic, watercolor, digital, scenic, realism, and so on. For the past few years, I've found calligraphy and letter art to be the most satisfying and stress relieving, hence those are my main pieces of art displayed here.
